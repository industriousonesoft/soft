Equalizer for Google Music

Equalizer for Google Music is the best Google Music player experience possible on you Mac!

Here is the main features:

Main Window && Mini Player

  • The original interface without Adobe Flash Player
  • Google Play All Access Support
  • Full Screen Support
  • Back/Forward/Refresh Support
  • Simplistic and beautiful
  • With Controls and Song Info

Equalizer

You can simply dial in the audio that suits you best or shape your sound at your fingertips without delay!

  • Build-in 10-Band & 31-Band equalizer and presets supported
  • Save the customized preset with a new name.

  • 24 handcrafted equalizers and presets supported.

Last.fm

You can scrobble any song of Google Music to Last.fm!

  • last.fm Scrobbling and Now Playing support

Preferences Setting

  • Customizable shortcuts Support
  • Show desktop notifications on track change Support
  • Launch app on startup Support
  • Keep app above other apps Support

Note:
Please note that Google Music is not yet available in all countries. You must have used your Google Music at least once to activate it while in one of the supported countries(eg: USA, Canada), otherwise you will not be able to use it.
